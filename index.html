Welcome to the CocoaCrunch!

Indulge in the perfect blend of creamy milk chocolate and crispy rice for a delightful crunch! Cocoa Crunch is crafted with Rainforest Alliance certified cocoa, ensuring sustainable farming practices and excellent quality.

Features

  • Made with Rainforest Alliance certified cocoa.
  • Perfect balance of milk chocolate and crispy rice.
  • Great for sharing or enjoying solo!

Nutrition Facts

  • 220 calories per bar.
  • 12g of fat, including 7g saturated fat.
  • 21g of sugar with 2g of protein.
